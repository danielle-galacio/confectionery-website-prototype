About us

Coco's Chocolatiers is a Canadian chocolatier and confectionery company founded in 1975 and known for its chocolate bars, sweets, pastries, and other desserts. Using the best local ingredients, Coco's Chocolatiers offers more than 30 flavors of the most decadent chocolate bars.
